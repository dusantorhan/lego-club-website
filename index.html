What We Do

In our LEGO club, students work on exciting projects using LEGO sets and simple programming. They learn how things work, solve problems, and develop logical thinking.

Each lesson is based on hands-on learning, where children build models, test their ideas, and improve their designs step by step.

Our Main Activities

  • Building LEGO models
  • Learning basic programming
  • Solving creative challenges
  • Working in teams
