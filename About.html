Our Club

We are a LEGO Robotics Club at Naša škola poznania. This is our first year running the club, and we are excited to build a strong community of young creators.

The club is divided into two groups based on the First LEGO League (FLL) competition. This year, we participated in the competition for the first time, giving students a chance to experience teamwork, problem-solving and real project work.

Through this experience, students not only build LEGO models, but also learn how to work together, share ideas and present their solutions.

About Our LEGO Robotics Club

Our LEGO Robotics Club is designed for children who enjoy building, creating and discovering how things work. Through fun and interactive activities, students learn important skills in a natural and engaging way.

The club combines creativity with logical thinking. Students work with LEGO sets to build different models and explore basic principles of engineering and robotics. Each lesson brings new challenges that encourage curiosity and independent thinking.

We believe that the best way to learn is by doing. That is why our lessons are based on hands-on activities, where children build their own creations, test them and improve them step by step.

First LEGO League Principles

Our club follows the principles of the First LEGO League (FLL), which focuses on learning through teamwork, creativity and real-world problem=solving.

  • Teamwork and collaboration
  • Creativity and innovation
  • Problem-solving and critical thinking
  • Respect and fair play
  • Learning through hands-on experience

These principles help students develop not only technical skills, but also important life skills.

Our Approach

Students are guided step by step, but they are also encouraged to explore their own ideas and solutions. The club supports creativity, independence and confidence.

Our goal is not only to teach technical skills, but also to help children develop a positive attitude towards learning and discovering new things.
